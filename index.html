About the Studio

Elena Rose of Rose Handwovens creates handwoven textiles on a nineteenth century counterbalance floor loom. She works out of her home studio in Medford. She began weaving at age nine and continues to explore weave structures and techniques.

Elena works in 100% natural wool and cotton. Her inventory includes blankets, scarves, towels, napkins, and decorative wall hangings. Custom commissions are also available.
